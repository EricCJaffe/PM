# Ministry Playbook — Discovery Intake Form (Church & Nonprofit)

> Use this form during discovery to assess each ministry area against the Foundation Stone methodology.
> Complete one per ministry/department. The 6 pillars (Vision, People, Data, Processes, Meetings, Issues) are assessed for each.
> Answers feed into gap analysis, process mapping, KPIs, and automation opportunity scoring.
> Language is adapted for churches, ministries, and faith-based nonprofits.

---

## Pre-Discovery: Organization Overview

Complete once per engagement (not per ministry).

### Mission & Spiritual Foundation
- [ ] What is the church/organization's mission statement?
- [ ] What are the core values? Are they communicated clearly to the congregation/team?
- [ ] What is the vision for the next 1-3 years?
- [ ] Who is the primary audience you serve? (congregation, community, specific demographics)
- [ ] What makes this church/organization unique in your community?
- [ ] How would you describe the culture in one sentence?

### Organizational Structure
- [ ] Provide or sketch the org chart (pastoral staff, elders/board, ministry leaders, volunteers)
- [ ] How many paid staff? Part-time? Volunteer leaders?
- [ ] What ministries or departments exist? Who leads each one?
- [ ] How are decisions made? (senior pastor, elder board, committee, consensus?)
- [ ] Who is the primary decision-maker for this engagement?
- [ ] Is there a church management system (ChMS)? Which one? (Planning Center, Breeze, CCB, etc.)

### Current State
- [ ] What tools/software does the organization use? (list all — ChMS, email, social, giving, website, etc.)
- [ ] What is working well today?
- [ ] What are the top 3 frustrations or bottlenecks?
- [ ] Has the organization gone through any recent changes? (pastoral transition, growth, facility change)
- [ ] What has been tried before that didn't work?
- [ ] How many people attend weekly? Is that growing, stable, or declining?

### Layer 1: Mission Alignment (VISION)

*How well does this ministry's work connect to the church's mission and vision?*

- [ ] What is this ministry's primary purpose?
- [ ] How does this ministry serve the church's overall mission?
- [ ] What are this ministry's top 3 goals for the year?
- [ ] Are these goals written down and shared with the team?
- [ ] How does this ministry define success? (attendance, engagement, spiritual growth, community impact?)
- [ ] What would "thriving" look like for this ministry in 12 months?
- [ ] Is this ministry aligned with the church's current strategic priorities?

### Layer 2: Success Metrics (DATA)

*What numbers does this ministry track, and how?*

- [ ] What metrics or indicators does this ministry currently track? (attendance, signups, giving, volunteers, etc.)
- [ ] How are they measured? (spreadsheet, ChMS, manual count, not tracked?)
- [ ] How often are metrics reviewed? By whom?
- [ ] What should be tracked but isn't?
- [ ] Is there a dashboard or report? Who sees it?
- [ ] What information lives in someone's head that should be in a system?
- [ ] How do you know if this ministry is healthy?

### Layer 3: People & Organization (PEOPLE)

*Who serves, and is it clear what they do?*

- [ ] Does each volunteer/staff role have a written description?
- [ ] Are roles and responsibilities clear? Is there overlap or confusion?
- [ ] Is there an accountability structure? (who reports to whom)
- [ ] How are new volunteers recruited and onboarded?
- [ ] What training or equipping exists for volunteers?
- [ ] Are there any staffing or volunteer gaps?
- [ ] Who is the "go-to" person when something goes wrong? What happens when they're unavailable?
- [ ] How are volunteers appreciated and retained?

### Layer 4: Communication (MEETINGS)

*How does information flow within and across ministries?*

- [ ] What recurring meetings does this ministry have? (weekly, monthly, quarterly)
- [ ] What is the format/agenda for each meeting?
- [ ] Are meetings effective, or do they feel like a waste of time?
- [ ] What communication tools are used? (email, text, GroupMe, Slack, PCO, in-person)
- [ ] How does this ministry communicate with the congregation? (announcements, social media, email blasts)
- [ ] How does this ministry coordinate with other ministries?
- [ ] Is there a weekly team huddle or check-in?
- [ ] Is there a planning retreat or annual review?
- [ ] Where do things fall through the cracks?

### Layer 5: Processes (PROCESSES)

*How is ministry work actually done week-to-week?*

- [ ] What are the top 5-10 recurring tasks or workflows in this ministry?
- [ ] Are any of these documented? (checklists, runbooks, how-to guides)
- [ ] What is the most manual or tedious task?
- [ ] What task takes the most time each week?
- [ ] What happens on a typical Sunday? Walk through the flow from setup to teardown.
- [ ] Are there handoffs to other ministries? How do they work?
- [ ] What happens when the key leader or volunteer is absent?
- [ ] What processes are paper-based or done in spreadsheets?
- [ ] What approval workflows exist? (budget, events, communications)
- [ ] How are events planned and executed?

### Layer 6: Pain Points (ISSUES)

*What's broken, frustrating, or draining energy?*

- [ ] What are the top 3 frustrations in this ministry?
- [ ] What tasks do people dread doing?
- [ ] Where do mistakes or miscommunication happen most often?
- [ ] What takes too long?
- [ ] What problems keep coming back?
- [ ] Are there any safety, compliance, or child protection concerns?
- [ ] What causes volunteer burnout in this area?
- [ ] If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing, what would it be?

### Layer 7: Automation & Technology Opportunities (PROCESSES + DATA)

*What could technology, automation, or AI improve?*

- [ ] Which repetitive tasks could be automated? (follow-up emails, scheduling, reminders)
- [ ] Are there data entry tasks that could be eliminated?
- [ ] What reports are created manually that could be auto-generated?
- [ ] What notifications or reminders should be automatic? (volunteer reminders, follow-up with guests)
- [ ] What integrations between tools are missing? (ChMS ↔ email, giving ↔ accounting, website ↔ events)
- [ ] What information do people have to look up in multiple places?
- [ ] Could AI help with any tasks? (sermon notes, social media, follow-up messages, data analysis)
- [ ] What would save the most volunteer hours if automated?

## Ministry-Specific Add-Ons

*Check the sections below that apply to this engagement and complete during discovery.*

### Worship & Production
- [ ] What is the current worship planning process? (PCO Services, spreadsheet, verbal)
- [ ] How are volunteers scheduled for services?
- [ ] What AV/production systems are in use?
- [ ] Is there a song database or set list archive?
- [ ] How are rehearsals coordinated?

### Kids & Youth Ministry
- [ ] What check-in system is used? (KidCheck, PCO Check-Ins, paper)
- [ ] What is the child protection / background check process?
- [ ] How are parents communicated with?
- [ ] What curriculum is used? How is it selected?
- [ ] How are small groups or classes organized by age?

### Small Groups / Life Groups
- [ ] How are groups organized and tracked?
- [ ] How do people sign up for groups?
- [ ] How are group leaders recruited, trained, and supported?
- [ ] What reporting exists? (attendance, engagement, leader health)
- [ ] How often do groups launch, end, or restructure?

### Giving & Stewardship
- [ ] What giving platform is used? (Tithe.ly, Pushpay, PCO Giving, etc.)
- [ ] How is giving data reconciled with accounting?
- [ ] Are giving statements sent automatically?
- [ ] Is there a stewardship campaign or annual pledge process?
- [ ] How are first-time givers followed up with?

### Guest Follow-Up
- [ ] How are first-time guests identified? (connect card, digital form, verbal)
- [ ] What is the follow-up process? (email, text, call, visit)
- [ ] How quickly does follow-up happen?
- [ ] What is the path from guest → regular attender → member → serving?
- [ ] Is the guest journey documented or tracked?
